Welcome to Saras Sensory Store Pvt Ltd

Saras Sensory Store Pvt Ltd is committed to empowering children with special needs through high-quality sensory products, toys, and educational aids for children with Autism, ADHD, and other neurodiverse needs. We develop and supply tools that help families, therapists, and educators promote learning, self-regulation, and life skills.

Expanding on our mission, we are proud to introduce our latest digital initiative — the Sarya ADL App (Activities of Daily Living), an interactive life skills training app designed specifically for Indian families. Sarya supports children in building daily routines and independence through culturally familiar, age-appropriate, and engaging activities.
